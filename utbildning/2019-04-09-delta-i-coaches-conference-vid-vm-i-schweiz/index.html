Delta i Coaches Conference vid VM i Schweiz

BWF arrangerar tränarkonferens i samband med VM i Basel 22-23 augusti. Programmet kommer att presenteras inom kort men nedan finns lite information om innehåll och föredragshållare.

Intresserade deltagare anmäler sig direkt via BWF:s hemsida. Badminton Sweden är inte involverad i anmälan eller researrangemang.

Intresserade föreningar kan söka utbildningsbidrag. Kontakta föreningsutvecklare Claes Johansson senast 15 maj för mer information. claes.johansson@badminton.nu

Läs mer om tränarkonferensen på Badminton World Federations hemsida.

World-class speakers will cover a variety of topics both on and off the court, including:

  • Elite Badminton Coaching
  • Sports Coaching
  • Para-Sport
  • Sport Science

Allistair McCaw

World-renowned Sports Performance Consultant and Mindset Coach. Over the past 24 years, he has worked with numerous Olympians, Paralympians, World Champion Athletes and no less than 11 Grand Slam Champions. 

Lars Uhre

Lars is one of the most accomplished badminton coaches worldwide, having worked with the Danish, Finnish, Dutch and New Zealand national teams, as well as being head coach of the International Badminton Academy.

Penny Briscoe OBE

Penny Briscoe OBE is the Director of Sport at the British Paralympic Association (BPA) Penny was Chef de Mission of the Great Britain Paralympic teams at Sochi 2014, Rio 2016 and PyeongChang 2018 and will again assume that duty at Tokyo 2020. 

Kim Myo Jung

Kim has been involved in development of Para badminton in Korea for over 20 years. Currently the Head coach of Korea’s Para-Badminton National Representative Team, Kim has coached Korean athletes to no less than 30 world and International titles, particularly in the Wheelchair Sport Classes.

Coaches registering for the BWF World Coaching Conference 2019 will receive the following: 

  • Admission to the BWF World Coaching Conference on 22nd and 23rd August 2019
  • Refreshments 22nd August 2019- Tea/Coffee/Juice/Water- Finger Food (sandwiches & Pastries)
  • Refreshments 23rd August 2019- Tea/Coffee/Juice/Water- Finger Food and Lunch (Salads, Soups, Meat & Vegetarian Dishes)
  • One ticket to attend the TOTAL BWF World Championships 2019 on 22nd (morning session) and 23rd August 2019 (evening session)
  • Yonex Delegate Bag, T-Shirt & Water Bottle

 Cost – €200.00
