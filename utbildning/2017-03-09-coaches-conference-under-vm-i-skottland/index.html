Coaches conference under VM i Skottland

BWF arrangerar tränarkonferens i samband med VM i Glasgow. Tema: The Journey to Elite Performance. Konferensen vänder sig till tränare på alla nivåer.

TOTAL BWF World Championships 2017

– Location:  Scotland, Glasgow – 25 & 26 August 2017
– Focus:  The Journey to Elite Performance
– Cost:   £157-£175 GBP

The next BWF World Coaching Conference will be held in Glasgow on 25 and 26 August 2017, in conjunction with the TOTAL BWF World Championships.

The subject of the conference is "The Journey to Elite Performance" and whether you are coaching high performance or grass roots players, it presents a perfect opportunity to develop and challenge your thinking.

Läs mer på Badminton World Federations hemsida.

För eventuella bidragsansökningar för kursavgiften, kontakta föreningsutvecklare Claes Johansson.
