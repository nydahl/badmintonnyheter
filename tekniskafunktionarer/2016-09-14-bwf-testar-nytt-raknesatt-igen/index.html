BWF testar nytt räknesätt igen

Nu testar BWF det nygamla räknesättet 5 x11 igen. Men med den stora skillnaden att det skall testas olika typer av förlängning vid ställningen 10-10.

BWF is in the process of testing the experimental scoring system (i.e. best of five games to 11 points with either setting at 10-10 to 15 points, or first to 13 points at 10-10) between September 2016 and December 2017.

The following is a list of confirmed tournaments which will be testing during this time period. Should more tournaments be added, we will keep you updated.
