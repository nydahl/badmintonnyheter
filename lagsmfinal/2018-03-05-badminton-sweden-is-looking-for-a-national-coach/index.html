Badminton Sweden is looking for a national coach

The Swedish Badminton Association is looking for a national coach for the senior national team with a location at Eurocenter in Uppsala from August 2018. Eurocenter is the Swedish badminton national training center located in Uppsala since 2014.

The national coach is responsible for the implementation of the management plan in accordance with the goals established by the sports director based on Badminton Sweden's guidelines.

The objectives of the center are:

  • To train/coach the players on a daily basis in a high-quality training environment so they can perform at their best level at the European Championships, World Championships and Olympic Games.
  • That Badminton Sweden in the short and long terms reaches the set sporting goals.

Required Qualifications

  • Coach education or equivalent international license/diploma/certification.
  • Documented CV with experience of developing players in training to international top level.
  • Professional competence and methodological expertise in high-performance badminton.
  • Experience of implementing training plans in a high-performance setting.
  • Team player, who has a desire to work and collaborate with colleagues and has the required communication skills.
  • Be able to create a motivating and developing training environment.
  • Fluent English speaking.

Tasks/responsibility

  • Responsibility for the national team players.
  • Prepare training plans, tournament plans and activities.
  • Responsibility for annual planning of training, competition calendars and other activities.
  • Deliver on court training sessions on a daily basis.
  • Reporting to Sports Director and Chief Executive in accordance with established procedures. Continuous development of Eurocenter concepts and infrastructure.
  • Selection of players for national team and Championship tournaments
  • Collaborate with junior national coaches.
  • Regular communication with national team players and their coaches not located at Eurocenter.
  • Stay up to date in general regarding Swedish elite players, clubs, tournaments etc.
  • Monitor individual players performance development.
  • Coach players during tournaments.
  • Responsibility for keeping the economy according to established budget frameworks from the sports director.
