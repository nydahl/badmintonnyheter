Tryck Internationella tävlingar

Uppmärksamma vilka regler som gäller när det gäller tryck vid deltagande på Internationella Seniortävlingar.

Det kommer att bli straffavgifter för de som inte deltar med rätt tryck på kläder vid de Internationella Seniortävlingarna.

Följande regler gäller från BEC:

As from 1 September 2013, Badminton Europe will issue administrative fines to the Member Associations who have players violating the clothing regulations in the Circuit Regulations and will impose penalties/fines to the Member Associations who have players violating the clothing regulations at the various European Championships.

GCR 22: Lettering on player’s clothing

 

-       PLAYER NAME

  • MUST HAVE a height of minimum 6 cm and maximum 10 cm
  • If used, the player's name shall include the family name (or an abbreviation thereof) and, if desired, the initials of the given name(s) or nickname and shall correspond with the name on the entry.
  • Please pay close attention to when the player name is mandatory (see Circuit Regulations and specific Event regulations)
  • Player name is MANDATORY in all main draw matches in International Challenge tournament, but it is OPTIONAL in qualification matches
  • Player name is MANDATORY in Semi-finals and Finals in International Series and Future Series tournaments, but it is OPTIONAL in all other matches

-       COUNTRY NAME

  • MUST HAVE a height of 5 cm exactly
  • If used, shall be either full country name in English or Olympic approved abbreviation. (e.g. Spain must be “SPAIN” or “ESP”, but cannot be “ESPANA”)
  • Please pay close attention to when the player name is mandatory (see specific Event regulations)

-       Lettering sequence and use of the advertisement:

  • The sequence on the shirt from top to bottom must be –
  1. Player name (if present), (6-10 cm in height)
  2. Country name (if present), (5 cm in height)
  3. Advert (if present). This advert is considered as “band of uniform”, and can therefore have a maximum height of 5 cm.

No other order is allowed
