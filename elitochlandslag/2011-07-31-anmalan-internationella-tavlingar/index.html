Anmälan internationella tävlingar

Nya regler om anmälningar till Internationella tävlingar från BEC/BWF:

 

BEC har bestämt att anmälningar till alla internationella tävlingar, skall ske

av förbundet via BWF:s online entry system.

 

Anmälningar online till (Level 1-3 Superserie, Grand Prix)  har funnits tidigare, men nu inkluderas även Level 4 tävlingar (Int. challenge, Int. series och Future serie tävlingar).

Detta innebär att vi förbundet enbart kan anmäla svenska spelare (medborgare) som har ett IBF-nr. som visar en svensk tillhörighet.

Information BWF Datum 2011-07-15

Online Entries – Level 4 Events

As part of BWF’s ongoing development, the online entry system will be used for Level 4 tournaments (International Challenge, International Series and Future Series), however this will be done in a phased implementation process.Tournaments that are part of the Badminton Europe circuit will be the first Level 4 tournaments to use the online entry system. Tournaments from other Continental Confederations are expected to be implemented in the system in the coming months. 

Doubles Events – Partner Required

In doubles events, the online entry system has the functionality of ‘partner required’. The Member Association should submit the other Member Association’s players name in the online entry system. The system will send an automated request to the other Member Association to accept or deny your entry.

By close of the deadline if the Member Association denies or did not confirm the entry, that entry will not be included in that event.

 

Mer information finns under BEC Badminton Circuit regulations paragraf 16.4, 16.5, 16.51 och 16.6
