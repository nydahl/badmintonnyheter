Ang 6-Nations och datum

Datumet är nu satt till den 27-30 april 2017.

Det har varit olika datum som figuerat kring 6-nationers för U17 2017.
Det blir i Kolding samtidigt med senior EM. 

"We have found what we think is an unique solution regarding dates for the 6 Nations U17 2017: We combine the Senior European Championships and 6 Nations U17 I the city of Kolding, situated in the southern part of Jutland! We´ll share the same venue (3 halls), set up, transportation, hotels etc. The players and all team staff members will get accreditation to the European Championships as well. Being part of such an event, I think, will be a great experience for all the players!"

We are therefore happy to invite you all to 6 Nations U17 in Denmark on the 27-30. April 2017 in the city of Kolding.
