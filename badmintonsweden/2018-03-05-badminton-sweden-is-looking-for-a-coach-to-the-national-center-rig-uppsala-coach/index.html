Badminton Sweden is looking for a coach to the national center/RIG Uppsala coach

The Swedish Badminton Association is looking for a coach to the national center (50%) and a coach of the RIG Uppsala (50%).

The Swedish Badminton Association, together with the Celsius School/Uppsala municipality, is looking for a center coach (50%) to the Eurocenter (National Center) in Uppsala and a coach of the RIG (Sport Gymnasium/Riksidrottsgymnasiet) business (50%). Together a full time employment.

The objectives of the service are:

Coach national center

  • To coach the players on a daily basis at the National Center together with the National coach, so that they can perform at their best level at the European Championships, World Championships and the Olympic Games.
  • That the federation reaches as many of the set sporting goals as possible.

Coach RIG (Gymnasium/high school/RIG Uppsala)

  • That the business follow the goals set in the development plan.
  • Develop young people who want to make both on an international elite career in badminton and want to achieve good learning outcomes.

Required Qualifications

  • Trainer education or documented equivalent skills are necessary, but other education can be taken into consideration. Great emphasis will be placed on personal suitability.
  • Confirmed CV with positive development work in training with players at U19 and senior level.
  • Features that provide great skills in developing badminton players. You should be a good educator and driven by educating and developing young people.
  • Experience in implementing training plans and development in a high performing training environment.
  • A team player who has good communication skills, dedication and social skills.
  • Be able to create a motivating and developing training environment.
  • Fluent English speaking.
  • You should have good organizational skills and be able to plan, implement and evaluate activities in the short and long term. You should be used to flexible and independent work with great responsibility.

Tasks/responsibility

  • Exercise workout on a daily basis (Monday-Friday) at the center / RIG. Flexible working hours, as well as national and international competitions are in the planning.
  • Communicate / report to the responsible persons for the national center at Celsiusskolan (RIG).
  • Run the business at RIG together with a colleague. Continuously work with RIG development networks and infrastructure development.
  • Individual development plans and evaluations of the players at the RIG / Center.
  • Coach on national and international competitions according to schedule for the RIG / national team.
  • Ensure that the activities at RIG hold the requirements stated by The Swedish Sports Confederation.
